About Us

Bean & Brew is a local independant coffee shop dedicated to serving freshly prepared food, quality coffee, and providing a warm and welcoming environment. We believe every cup of coffee should bring a smile and a moment of relaxation to your day.

Beyond great coffee and food, Bean & Brew is a space for community. We're proud to host Little Beans, our free weekly mum and baby group, offering a rellaxed and welcoming place for mums to connect while babies relax nearby.

We also run the Beans & Brew Book club, bringing people together over good books, good coffee and good conversation. With current members aging from 19 - 74yrs come along enjoy the stories off the pages as well as on them, everyone is welcome.

More details can be found on our events page, ask instore for full details.
